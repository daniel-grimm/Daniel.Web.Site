Technology

I have an interest in a variety of technologies ranging from the distro-hopping to space.

Linux + Open Source

I use Linux on my personal computers and have settled on the Solus Budgie and KDE OpenSuse Tumbleweed distros. I enjoy them for different reasons but enjoy both equally.

I enjoy OpenSuse for a variety of reasons primarily being that it's a rolling distribution very close to upstream highlighting the KDE desktop. I also like the tools that OpenSuse offers ranging from large applications like it's build systems that it makes available to developers all the way down to tools like YAST2.

Solus Budgie edition I enjoy for it's beautiful visuals and dark theme by default. It's the Gnome desktop perfected and I enjoy the idea of applets although I mainly use them on Ubuntu Budgie instead of Solus Budgie. I also enjoy the rolling nature of the OS, meaning that I don't have to keep up with the major releases since I frequently stop using my computers for extended periods of time. I can leave them and run a eopkg up and know that my system will be updated to the latest without requiring an extensive upgrade to a new major version.
